For thge fig-swirl: Melt butter over medium heat in a saucepan. add brown suger and stir to dissolve. halve all of the figs and toss in a saucepan with water and lemon juice. cook over medium heat, srtiffing frequently, untill you have a chucky-jammy mixture. Add alt with one of two stirs, set aside and let cool completly.

Ice Cream: In a small pot over medium heat, combine milk, and granulated sugar until sugar is compleatly dissolved and the milk is just barley lukewarm. Whisk in the egg yolks. Set mixture aside in the fridge and wait until the fig mixture is cooled.

Using an icecream machine, pour liquids into the frozen basin and process according to manufacture instructions, i.e., let spin and thicken for 20 minutes before adding mascarpone, fig jam mixture, and the nuts. Continue to process for +/- 10 minutes. Pour semi-frozen mixture into a pyrex dish or glass tupperware. Freeze for at least 2 hours before serving.

  • ▢ 1 1/2 cups milk
  • ▢ 1/2 cups mascarpone
  • ▢ 1/2 tsp pink salk
  • ▢ 1lb Black Mission Figs
  • ▢ 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • ▢ 2-4 tbsp water
  • ▢ 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • ☑ 1/3 granulated sugar
  • ☑ 2 egg yolks
  • ▢ 1 lemon, jucied
  • ▢ 2 tbsp butter
  • ▢ 1 cup honey roasted pecans, roughly
        chopped
